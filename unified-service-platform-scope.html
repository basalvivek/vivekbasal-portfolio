Service Pro
Management System

A full-stack CRM and operations platform built for field service businesses. It consolidates the entire customer journey — from the first enquiry through engineer visits, quotation, order confirmation, production, installation scheduling, and payment collection — into a single, role-aware web application.

Module 1 Complete Role-Based Access (Admin / Staff) React + Node.js + PostgreSQL Email Notifications via Gmail SMTP 6 Modules Planned
a

Scope

Customer Enquiry Management
Log, assign, and track enquiries from first contact through quote, confirmation, and conversion to order. Full follow-up log history per enquiry with channel and status tracking.
Engineer Visit Scheduling
Schedule, reschedule, complete, or cancel site visits per enquiry. Assign engineers, set time slots and durations, and track visit status end-to-end with automated email notifications.
Order & Payment Management
Convert confirmed enquiries to orders. Track total amount, deposit, and balance payments. Record instalments with payment type, date, and method. Full payment history and pending payment views.
Employee & Department Management
Manage employees with auto-generated codes (EMP-0001), assign to multiple departments (Sales, Design, Installation, Manufacturing, Management), and control role-based access across the platform.
Dashboard & Reporting
Admin dashboard with KPI cards for enquiry counts, order pipeline value, today's visits, and upcoming installations. Reports covering revenue, employee performance, enquiry sources, and trends over time.
Automated Email Notifications
Gmail SMTP-powered transactional emails triggered on key events — visit scheduled/rescheduled/completed, order confirmations, and status updates — keeping customers and staff informed automatically.

1
Customer Contacts — Enquiry Logged Staff logs the enquiry at /enquiries/new. Auto-code ENQ-0001 assigned. Customer record created (CUST-0001) or matched from existing. Product interest, channel (Phone/Email/Walk-in/Website), priority, and budget estimate recorded.
2
Assign & Status → In Progress Admin assigns enquiry to a staff member. Status updated to In Progress. Follow-up logs added per interaction — each log captures channel, notes, and optional status change.
3
Visit Scheduled → Engineer Surveys Site Admin creates a visit from the enquiry. Engineer assigned, date and time slot set. Status → Visit Scheduled. Email notification dispatched to customer. Visit appears on the engineer's Daily Schedule and admin Calendar view.
4
Visit Completed → Quote Sent Engineer completes visit; admin marks it Complete. Enquiry status moves to Visit Done, then Quote Sent once the quotation is delivered to the customer (via email or in person).
5
Quote Accepted → Enquiry Confirmed → Order Created Customer confirms. Enquiry status → Confirmed. Admin clicks "Convert to Order" — an order record (ORD-00001) is created linked to the enquiry and customer. Enquiry status → Converted to Order.
6
Deposit Collected → Production Begins Deposit payment recorded against the order. Order status → Deposit Paid → In Production. Balance payment amount and expected installation date tracked throughout.
7
Ready to Install → Installation Scheduled → Installed → Completed Order progresses through production. Installation scheduled via the visit module. Final balance recorded upon completion. Order status → Completed. Full audit trail preserved in order logs and payment records.
