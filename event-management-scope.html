Proof of Concept · Community Management

United Digambar Jain
Community System

A full-stack charitable trust management web application built for internal administrators of the United Digambar Jain Community. It streamlines the complete lifecycle of event operations — from creation and scheduling to member registration, activity assignments, rehearsal tracking, and financial management.

Version 1.0.0 Internal Admin Users Desktop Web App 12 Modules Completed
a

Scope

Event Lifecycle Management
End-to-end creation, scheduling, venue coordination, and closure of community events with date-time pickers and PDF export.
Member Registration
Register Core Members, Team Leads, Participants, Volunteers, and Attendees with role-based filtering, QR code generation, and exportable member cards.
Activity Assignment & Tracking
Define categorised activities per event, bulk-assign members, and track status transitions from Pending ? In-Progress ? Completed with timestamps.
Financial & Donation Management
Track partner organisation participation with donation commitments, installment payment recording (Cash, Cheque, Bank Transfer, Card), and balance calculation.
Rehearsal Scheduling
Schedule practice sessions per activity, record attendance, track time-in/time-out, flag absences, and compute attendance percentages per member.
Progress Dashboard
Real-time activity completion views, status distributions, member participation rates, overdue alerts, and PDF progress reports per event.
